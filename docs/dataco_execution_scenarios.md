# DataCo Execution-Risk Scenarios

DataCo is used to measure execution risk through late delivery behavior. The dataset provides an empirical view of how delivery risk varies across shipping mode, market, region, country, and product context when those fields are available.

The project uses DataCo to construct data-informed execution-risk scenarios rather than to directly estimate the exact economic cost of execution violations in the demand-planning datasets. Global and context-level late delivery rates provide evidence that execution feasibility varies across supply-chain conditions, and percentile anchors from the context-level distribution are mapped to `lambda_execution` values through a transparent configurable formula.

Configured values are used only as fallbacks when DataCo-derived anchors are unavailable. Any generated values should be interpreted as dataset-derived sensitivity weights, not universal late-delivery rates or exact economic estimates.

The purpose of these scenarios is to test whether the preferred demand-planning strategy changes when execution burden becomes more important.

| scenario_name | DataCo risk anchor | lambda_execution | source | interpretation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| baseline | No late-delivery percentile anchor | Generated from the configured base lambda, or fallback value if DataCo is unavailable | `dataco_derived` or `config_fallback` | Baseline execution-risk sensitivity for comparison. |
| dataco_low | 30th percentile of context-level late-delivery rates | Generated by the configured mapping formula | `dataco_derived` or `config_fallback` | Lower execution-risk sensitivity. |
| dataco_median | 50th percentile of context-level late-delivery rates | Generated by the configured mapping formula | `dataco_derived` or `config_fallback` | Typical observed execution-risk sensitivity. |
| dataco_high | 75th percentile of context-level late-delivery rates | Generated by the configured mapping formula | `dataco_derived` or `config_fallback` | Higher execution-risk sensitivity. |
| dataco_severe | 95th percentile of context-level late-delivery rates | Generated by the configured mapping formula | `dataco_derived` or `config_fallback` | Severe execution-risk sensitivity. |

The generated scenario table records `scenario_name`, percentile anchor, late-delivery-rate anchor, `lambda_execution`, mapping formula, source, and fallback status for reproducibility.
